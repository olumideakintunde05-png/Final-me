Daniel Wisdom Hub was created with one purpose: to put timeless wisdom in your pocket. In a world full of noise, we believe the right words at the right moment can shift a perspective, spark a decision, or carry someone through a hard day.

Every quote on this platform has been carefully curated — drawn from the greatest thinkers, leaders, poets, and philosophers in human history — and organised to be discoverable exactly when you need it.

Privacy Policy
Effective date: 1 January 2026
1. Overview

Daniel Wisdom Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Usage Data — Pages visited, quotes viewed, categories browsed. Collected anonymously to improve content recommendations.
  • Device Data — Browser type, operating system, screen resolution for rendering optimisation. No personally identifiable information included.
  • Notification Preferences — If you enable push notifications, your browser generates a subscription token used only to deliver your requested reminders.
  • Saved Quotes — Stored locally on your device using localStorage. We do not transmit or store your saved quotes on our servers.
  • Settings — Theme, font size, reminder times, and other preferences are stored locally on your device only.
3. How We Use Your Information
  • To deliver daily quote notifications at your chosen times.
  • To remember your preferences across sessions (theme, font size, saved quotes).
  • To analyse anonymous usage patterns and improve the platform.
  • To detect and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • OneSignal — Push notification delivery. See onesignal.com/privacy_policy
  • Google Fonts — Font loading via fonts.googleapis.com. No personal data is shared.
  • Unsplash — Quote background images served directly from Unsplash CDN.
5. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage exclusively to store your preferences (theme, saved quotes, notification settings) on your own device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

6. Your Rights
  • Clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via Settings or your browser's notification settings.
  • Request information about any data we hold about you.
  • Have any personal data deleted upon request.
7. Data Security

All connections to Daniel Wisdom Hub are encrypted via HTTPS. Notification tokens are transmitted over secure channels and stored with restricted access.

8. Children's Privacy

Daniel Wisdom Hub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date at the top will be updated. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
